<p>We had a wonderful lab party just before the Christmas break! As a tradition, we have piñatas :) Such a fun experience!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>We enjoyed the random wiggling piñata, the blindfolded clumsiness, the "strategic brute force," and, best of all, bursting the candy-filled piñata! At that moment, I started to think that hitting a piñata is reminiscent of a research project. Yes, we are frustrated from time to time. Yes, when we are blindfolded, we often doubt our ability to achieve anything. Yet, we have to blindly trust that there will always be a happy ending—one filled with a variety of candies. Which one do you want to pick?</p>
