<p>Time flies... 2021 has been unexpectedly busy. In somewhy, I miss the COVID time. It seemed the time machine suddenly slowed down. Without many commitments and meetings, I enjoyed much-needed quiet time to read and think... With 2020 is in the rare mirror, we are back to a fast-paced "modern" society again. The calendar was filled quickly (yet again!). While we are focusing on chasing the preset goals, we should not forget the beautiful things quietly brooding around us. Looking through the window from our lab, the refreshing "pink" signals the exciting Spring is coming (finally to mid-west!). People sometimes ask me "how do you enjoy the weather in the mid-west? Too much snow, isn't it?" Yup, actually we love the weather here. To be precise, we love the excitement of the seasonal changes that mother nature brings to us four times a year. That really makes life interesting. We are "kids" who are constantly longing for lasting beauty throughout seasons. </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
